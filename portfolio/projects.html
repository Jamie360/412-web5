Advertising poster

For this project, I created an advertising poster for the restaurant La Notté. La Notté is a kosher Italian food restaurant in Montreal. I chose to create an advertising poster for La Notté because it was a new restaurant that did not have any public advertisements at the time. The poster that I created has a black background with an image of a table set with an elegant water pitcher and wine glass in its center. I used a simple white font that is easy to read and which highlights the Italian flag to emphasize that it is an Italian restaurant.
