About Us

Mecanergie Inc. started in 1979 servicing plumbing, heating and air-conditioning systems for the Greater Montreal area. They started working in commercial, industrial and residential sectors where they continue to this day. Mecanergie has certification and licenses and is a member of the CMMTQ, RBQ, ACQ, AECQ, CCQ, and CETAF. The company has a team of certified technicians whose outstanding skills go hand in hand with the exceptional customer service they provide. Clients know that Mecanergie Inc. stands for integrity and they can rely on our fair work assessments. Call on Mecanergie's team for all of your residential, industrial and commercial plumbing, heating and air-conditioning needs.
