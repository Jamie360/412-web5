Six Word Story

The six-word story was a project for my Graphic Design class last year. The project was to design a poster of a quote that we liked. The quote that I chose is “books take you to faraway places”. For the poster design, I chose a simple dark background with a woman reading a book. The poster’s design has various book pages with literary images including Hogwarts from Harry Potter, the Mad Hatter hat, a mermaid, pirate ship and a dragon. I used a yellow cursive font that creates contrast and coordinates with the black background.
